# Gold Jewelry Export Rules (India to UAE)

## Documentation Required

- Commercial Invoice
- Packing List
- Certificate of Origin
- Hallmark Certification
- Bill of Entry (for imports)

---

## UAE VAT

UAE VAT on gold jewelry is typically 5%.

Investment-grade gold (99% purity or higher) may be VAT exempt.

---

## Import Duty

Gold jewelry imports into UAE may attract customs duty depending on classification.

HS Code commonly used for gold jewelry:
7113 – Articles of Jewelry and Parts Thereof

---

## Payment Methods

Common payment methods in gold trade:

- Letter of Credit (LC)
- Advance TT
- DP at sight

---

## Compliance Considerations

- Must comply with Dubai Municipality standards.
- Proper declaration of gold purity required.
- Anti-money laundering regulations apply for high-value transactions.
