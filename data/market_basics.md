# Gold Market Basics

## What Affects Gold Prices

1. US Dollar strength
2. Inflation rates
3. Central bank reserves
4. Geopolitical uncertainty
5. Interest rates

Gold prices usually move inversely to the US Dollar.

---

## Spot vs Futures

Spot Price:
Current market price for immediate delivery.

Futures Price:
Agreed price for delivery at a future date.

Jewelry pricing usually uses spot price.

---

## Currency Impact

In India:
Gold price in INR =
International gold price (USD) × USD/INR exchange rate

Currency volatility directly affects domestic gold prices.

---

## Market Volatility

During economic crises:
- Gold demand increases
- Prices rise due to safe-haven demand

During strong economic growth:
- Gold demand may decrease
